Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Choreable ("the App") is a family chore tracking app built for personal use. This policy explains what data the App collects and how it is handled.

Data We Collect

The App stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • Family member names and avatars you enter
  • Chore names, schedules, and point values you create
  • Chore completion records and approval history
  • Point balances and dollar amounts

Data Sharing

All data is stored on-device and synced between your iPhone and Apple Watch using Apple's WatchConnectivity framework. No data is ever sent to any external server, third party, or us.

Third-Party Services

The App does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Children's Privacy

The App is designed for family use and does not collect any personal information from children. All data remains on your device and is never transmitted externally.

Data Deletion

You can delete all app data at any time by deleting the App from your device. This permanently removes all stored information.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a revised date.
